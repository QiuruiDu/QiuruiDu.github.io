About me

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student in Statistics at North Carolina State University, advised by Dr. Wenbin Lu.

Before this, I obtained my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Applied Statistics from Sun Yat-sen University and Fudan University in China, respectively.
